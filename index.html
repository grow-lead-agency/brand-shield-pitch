Brand Shield16 · Pillar 3
Three levels of action

From a warning to court

1

Warning letter in the right jurisdiction

The brand/sales manager submits, from their queue (watchlist / open case), that they want to send a warning. Either their legal team handles it — but it's in their queue, they see it happening — or they hand it to us / our partner law firm, which arranges delivery on the brand's behalf.

2

Sample order + recording the process

We deliberately order the product/sample from the given shop and record the entire process (order → delivery → unboxing). Physical proof, not just a screenshot.

3

Litigation

We represent — together with law firms. For smaller brands: if someone runs another brand, we advise them to use this too.

It stands on evidence from Pillar 2 (screenshot, timestamp, test purchase). The client chooses how far to go.
